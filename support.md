# Spell Write Support

**App:** Spell Write  
**Platform:** iOS / iPadOS  
**Category:** Education (Spelling + Handwriting Practice)

---

## App Summary
Spell Write helps users practice spelling and handwriting by listening to words, writing them by hand, and using OCR to check results. Users can track progress, export stats, and share seeded word-list challenges.

---

## Getting Started
1. Open the app and create or select a profile.
2. Choose a difficulty level.
3. Select Demo (free) or Full mode (paid unlock).
4. Start a session and begin writing words.

---

## How Gameplay Works
- Listen to the spoken word and optional definition.
- Write the word using your finger or Apple Pencil.
- Tap **"Read My Writing"** to preview recognition.
- Tap **Submit** to check your answer.

---

## Demo vs Full Lists
- **Demo:** Free sample words.
- **Full:** Complete word lists unlocked via in-app purchase.

---

## Buying and Restoring Purchases
- Select a locked Full list to purchase.
- Complete the Apple purchase flow.
- If content does not unlock:
  - Tap **Restore Purchases**
  - Ensure you are signed into the correct Apple ID

---

## Using Word-List Seeds
- Enter a seed to generate a reproducible word list.
- Share seeds with others to play identical runs.

---

## Managing Profiles
- Profiles store stats separately on-device.
- Create multiple profiles for different users.

---

## Understanding Stats and Exports
- Stats include attempts, correct answers, score, and session info.
- Stats are stored locally.
- You can export stats as a CSV and share via email.

---

## OCR / Handwriting Tips
- Write clearly with spacing between letters.
- Use **"Read My Writing"** before submitting.
- If recognition is incorrect, erase and rewrite.
- Check recognized text before submitting.

---

## Troubleshooting Purchases
- Confirm App Store account is signed in.
- Use **Restore Purchases**.
- Check network connection.
- Retry purchase if needed.
- Note: sandbox/test purchases may behave differently.

---

## Troubleshooting OCR Recognition
- Improve letter spacing and clarity.
- Avoid overlapping letters.
- Rewrite unclear characters.

---

## FAQ

**Do I need an account to use the app?**  
No. No login is required.

**Are my stats stored online?**  
No. Stats are stored locally on your device.

**How do I share a run?**  
Share the seed value and difficulty.

**My purchase didn’t unlock. What do I do?**  
Use Restore Purchases and confirm your Apple ID.
