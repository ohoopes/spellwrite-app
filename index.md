# Spell Write Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 17, 2026

## Data collection and storage
Spell Write does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

Gameplay statistics (such as words attempted, spelled correctly, and session stats) are stored locally on the user’s device.

## Data export (CSV + email)
Spell Write provides an optional feature that allows users to export their gameplay statistics as a CSV file and share it using the iOS Mail app. This export/sharing occurs only when the user chooses to do so. Spell Write does not send emails or transmit files on its own.

Once exported and emailed, the CSV file is handled by the user’s chosen email provider and Apple’s Mail app. Please review your email provider’s and Apple’s privacy policies for details.

## Third-party services
If you make purchases in Spell Write, the purchase process is handled by Apple. Apple may collect information as part of the purchase flow. See Apple’s privacy policy for details.

Spell Write does not include analytics, advertising, or other third-party tracking libraries.
